Thank you all for your support and desire to use our services

We appreciate your active effort to prevent disease and keep you and your family healthy with Lux Health. We absolutely understand your desire for at-home care. However, in times like these, we are not technologists or business people.

We are, first and foremost, health care providers. We believe we have a greater duty to temporarily reorient our time and service of healing in these trying times. In light of Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19), we will not be scheduling any more appointments at this time. We will temporarily not be available to answer our phones.

Do not catastrophize. This is an opportunity for us as a community to rise up to the challenge and act with our resilience and compassion.

We look forward to serving you again following this challenge.

The Lux Health Team
