Thank you for all of your support and excitement

We appreciate your active effort to prevent disease and keep you and your family healthy with Lux Health. We absolutely understand your desire for at-home care.

However, we are health care providers. We believe we have a greater duty to temporarily focus our service in these trying times. In light of Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19), we have decided to pause our medical services through Lux Health to volunteer our expertise and time combating the spread of COVID-19 more directly.

This unfortunately means we will not be available to answer our phones. All current appointments are canceled. Please check you emails for the notification.

Do not catastrophize. This is an opportunity for us as a community to rise up to the challenge and act with our resilience and compassion.

We look forward to serving you again following this challenge.

The Lux Health Team

If you are having a medical emergency, call 911.
